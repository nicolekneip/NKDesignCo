About

Hi, I’m Nicole, the creator behind NKDSGNS Co. — and a recent bride who fell in love with the magic of wedding planning!

After getting married in December, I realized how much joy I found in curating the perfect aesthetic, organizing every detail, and discovering unique ways to make the big day feel just right. From florals and signage to bridesmaid dresses and table decor, I wanted it all to be beautiful, meaningful, and accessible.

Here at NKDSGNS Co., I create trend-forward wedding vision boards that showcase everything from gowns and accessories to invitations and decor. Every item featured includes a direct link so you can shop the look easily — no more endless searching!

In addition to sharing inspiration, I design Canva wedding templates that are ready to personalize and download. Whether you’re a bride, planner, or helping out a friend, you'll find modern templates for:

  • Invitations

  • Welcome signs
  • Programs
  • Table numbers
  • Planners and more

My goal? To make wedding planning fun, stress-free, and stylish — for every couple and every vibe. Whether you’re planning a boho beach wedding, a modern minimalist ceremony, or something in between, I hope this space brings you clarity and joy along the way.

Thanks for being here — and cheers to creating a wedding that feels like you.

Nicole
Founder, NKDSGNS Co.
